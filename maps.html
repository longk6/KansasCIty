Mapping Kansas City's Schools Subheading

  1. Home
  2. Mapping it Visually

This is an interactive map of today's school district boundaries within Kansas City, MO only. Clicking on each dot will give additional information about the school.

This was supposed to be an interactive ARCgis map, however, I could not figure out how to embed it into the website properly.

The The green outline is Wyandotte County. The Yellow outline is Jackson County. The Pink outline is Johnson County. The red push pin within Jackson County is the middle of Kansas City, MO. While the Green push pin is Troost Avenue, where the dividing line is for the KCMSD and as a city general rule signifies the divide between the poorer and richer neighborhoods. The Green push pin in Johnson County is highlighting Overland Park, KS, an affluent suburb of Kansas City. Others on that list are Shawnee Mission and Olathe, KS - both also in Johnson County.
